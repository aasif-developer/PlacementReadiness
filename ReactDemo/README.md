A modern **Placement Readiness Self Assessment Portal** built using **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript** that helps students evaluate their placement preparedness through an interactive multi-step assessment. The portal generates a placement readiness score, highlights strengths and weaknesses, and provides a personalized roadmap for improvement.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Many students are unaware of their placement readiness until they attend campus interviews. Existing platforms mainly focus on individual areas such as coding, aptitude, or communication, forcing students to use multiple applications to evaluate themselves.

This project provides a **single platform** that evaluates multiple placement parameters and delivers a comprehensive readiness report with personalized recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Evaluate students' placement readiness.
- Identify strengths and improvement areas.
- Generate a personalized readiness report.
- Provide actionable career improvement suggestions.
- Help placement cells and training coordinators identify students who require additional guidance.

# 💡 Why This Project?

This project was developed to bridge the gap between academic learning and placement preparation by providing students with a unified platform to evaluate their readiness. Instead of relying on multiple websites for coding, aptitude, communication, and career guidance, students receive a comprehensive assessment in one place.
